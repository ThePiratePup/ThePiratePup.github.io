I'm Josiah Boucher, a computational media researcher and multimedia artist. I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the Computational Media program at Worcester Polytechnic Institute and a member of the AI Futures Lab. My research is a critical investigation of emerging computational media. I'm especially interested in creative and ethical inquiries surrounding generative AI, and elevating the voices of artists in this domain. I'm also a game developer working with Groundwater Studios, a team of international artists developing small-scale projects at a relaxed pace.
